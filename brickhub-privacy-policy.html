Privacy Policy

At Brickhub, we prioritize the privacy of our users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect personal data when using our application.

Information Collected

Through the Brickhub application, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal information such as name and email address.
  • Information related to the use of the application (e.g., favorite Lego sets, collection details).
  • Information about transactions and activities performed through the application.

Use of Information

The information we collect may be used for the following purposes:

  • To personalize and enhance the application experience.
  • To provide user support.
  • To communicate with and inform our users.
  • For analytical purposes, to evaluate and improve the performance of the application.

Protection of Information

Brickhub takes appropriate technical and administrative measures to protect your personal information. However, we cannot guarantee the complete security of data transmissions over the internet.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

Your personal information may only be shared with third parties in the following circumstances:

  • In compliance with legal obligations.
  • With our application service providers, solely for the purpose of providing services.

Children's Privacy

Brickhub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with personal information, they can contact us to have this information removed.

Changes to Privacy Policy

Brickhub may update this privacy policy from time to time. When changes are made, we will post the updated policy on this page. We encourage our users to regularly review this page for any changes.
